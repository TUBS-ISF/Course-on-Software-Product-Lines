# History and News

This file documents some major milestones regarding this project.

## 2026

- We created and published brand-new [exercise material](https://github.com/TUBS-ISF/Course-on-Software-Product-Lines-Exercises) accompanying the lecture under a creative commons license.

## 2025

- Our [paper on this course](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/SoftVarE-Group/Papers/main/2025/2025-VaMoS-Kuiter.pdf) has been accepted at this year's VaMoS being held in Rennes.
  Moreover, it was awarded the [Best Paper Award](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/SoftVarE-Group/Papers/main/2025/2025-VaMoS-Kuiter-BestPaperCertificate.pdf) at said conference.

## 2024

- The course was given at Paderborn University.
- The course was given for the first time at TU Braunschweig.

## 2023

- We published the first iteration of video recordings of our course on [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL4hJhdKDPIxha8So7muX2zfNUU8NBoiu3).
- The course was given at Harz University of Applies Sciences, Wernigerode.
- The course was given at Ulm University, replacing a previous course.
- The [first fork](https://github.com/rse-group/Course-on-Software-Product-Lines) of this project was created for use at the University of Indonesia.

## 2022

- We started to give this course for the first time in Bern and Magdeburg.
  In Magdeburg, this replaced a previous lecture on similar topics.

## 2021

- First commit in this repository.
